# Hardware Requirements

## Minimum Requirements

- **CPU**: 2 cores
- **RAM**: 4 GB
- **Storage**: 20 GB SSD
- **Network**: 10 Mbps

## Recommended

- **CPU**: 4+ cores
- **RAM**: 8 GB
- **Storage**: 50 GB SSD
- **Network**: 100 Mbps

## High Performance

- **CPU**: 8+ cores
- **RAM**: 16 GB
- **Storage**: 100 GB NVMe SSD
- **Network**: 1 Gbps

## Cloud Providers

### AWS
- t3.medium (minimum)
- t3.xlarge (recommended)
- c5.2xlarge (high performance)

### DigitalOcean
- 4 GB / 2 CPUs (minimum)
- 8 GB / 4 CPUs (recommended)

### Vultr
- 4 GB Cloud (minimum)
- 8 GB Cloud (recommended)
